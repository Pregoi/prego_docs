PDPA-Compliant Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how iPrego Pte. Ltd. collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal data in accordance with the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).

By using the Prego platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect personal data including:

### Account Information

- contact information
- company name
- email address
- name

### Usage Information

- activity logs
- device and browser information
- system usage data
- login records

### Customer Data

Customers may upload business information including:

- operational data
- financial information
- employee records

This data is processed as part of providing the platform services.

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

Personal data may be used for the following purposes:

- complying with legal obligations
- maintaining platform security
- improving service performance
- providing customer support
- verifying user identity
- providing and operating the Prego platform

## 4. Consent

By providing personal data, users consent to its collection and use for the purposes described in this policy.

Users may withdraw consent where permitted under PDPA by contacting us.

Withdrawal of consent may affect the ability to use certain services.

## 5. Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose personal data to:

- regulatory authorities where required by law
- professional advisors where necessary
- infrastructure providers and hosting services
- service providers assisting with platform operations

Such parties are required to maintain confidentiality and data protection standards.

## 6. Data Protection Measures

We implement reasonable security arrangements including:

- system monitoring
- access control mechanisms
- secure infrastructure
- encrypted data transmission

These measures help protect personal data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Customers may request deletion of personal data where applicable.

## 8. Access and Correction

Under the PDPA, individuals may request:

- correction of inaccurate data
- access to their personal data

Requests may be submitted to our Data Protection Officer.

## 9. International Data Transfers

As a cloud-based service, personal data may be processed outside Singapore.

Where this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to maintain PDPA standards.

## 10. Data Protection Officer

In accordance with PDPA requirements, iPrego Pte. Ltd. has appointed a Data Protection Officer (DPO).

For privacy inquiries or requests:

- **Email:** privacy@pregoi.com
- **Company:** iPrego Pte. Ltd.
- **Location:** Singapore

## 11. Updates to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The latest version will always be published on our website.
