## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how iPrego Pte. Ltd. uses cookies and similar technologies when users access the Prego website or platform.

## 2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on a user's device when visiting a website.

They help improve website functionality and user experience.

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

Required for the operation of the platform. Examples include:

- security controls
- session management
- authentication cookies

### Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how users interact with the platform. Examples include:

- performance monitoring
- page usage statistics

### Preference Cookies

Used to remember user settings such as:

- interface configuration
- language preferences

## 4. Managing Cookies

Users may control cookies through browser settings.

Disabling cookies may affect certain platform features.

## 5. Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in technology or regulations.
