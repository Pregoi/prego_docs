## Join the community

- **GitHub** — [github.com/prego](https://github.com/prego) — Code, issues, and discussions.
- **Documentation** — You're here. Use the sidebar and search to find guides and reference.

## Contact support

For support or partnership inquiries, reach out through your usual channel or the contact details provided by your Prego deployment.

## Contribute to docs

This docs site is open source. To fix a typo or suggest an improvement, open the prego_docs repo, edit the Markdown/MDX file for the page, and submit a pull request.
